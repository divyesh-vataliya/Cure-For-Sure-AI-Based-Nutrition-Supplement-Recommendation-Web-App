Nutrition in Your Body

Understanding how nutrition affects your body is the first step to better health. Our bodies require a balanced intake of:

  • Macronutrients (proteins, carbohydrates, fats)
  • Micronutrients (vitamins and minerals)
  • Water and other essential compounds

Each nutrient plays a vital role in maintaining your health and well-being.

Why Nutrition Matters

Proper nutrition is essential for:

  • Heart health and cardiovascular function
  • Brain function and cognitive performance
  • Bone strength and muscle maintenance
  • Immune system function
  • Energy production and metabolism
